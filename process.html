Our Process

Transparent, Collaborative, Excellence-Driven Project Delivery

How We Work

Our proven 7-step process ensures every project is delivered on time, on budget, and exceeds expectations

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, goals, budget, and timeline. Our team will assess your project requirements across all AECI disciplines and sectors.

  • Free initial consultation
  • Site assessment and analysis
  • Needs and goals discussion
  • Budget and timeline review
2

Design & Planning

Our architecture and engineering teams collaborate to create detailed designs and plans. We utilize cutting-edge technology including BIM, 3D visualization, and robotics simulation.

  • Conceptual design development
  • 3D modeling and visualization
  • Engineering calculations
  • Technology integration planning
3

Permitting & Approvals

We handle all permitting, zoning approvals, and regulatory compliance. Our team manages HOA approvals, building permits, and all necessary documentation.

  • Building permit applications
  • HOA and zoning approvals
  • Code compliance verification
  • Documentation preparation
4

Pre-Construction

Before breaking ground, we finalize all details including material selection, subcontractor coordination, and project scheduling. We can connect you with our marketplace for materials.

  • Material procurement
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Detailed scheduling
  • Site preparation planning
5

Construction & Execution

Our experienced construction teams bring your project to life with superior craftsmanship, robotics-enhanced precision, and rigorous quality control at every phase.

  • Daily quality inspections
  • Regular progress updates
  • Technology-enhanced construction
  • Safety protocol enforcement
6

Inspection & Quality Assurance

We conduct thorough inspections and address any issues before final completion. Our quality assurance process ensures every detail meets our high standards.

  • Comprehensive inspections
  • Punch list completion
  • Code compliance verification
  • Final walkthrough preparation
7

Project Completion & Support

We deliver your completed project with full documentation, warranties, and ongoing support. Our commitment to your satisfaction doesn't end at completion.

  • Final walkthrough and approval
  • Complete documentation delivery
  • Warranty information
  • Ongoing maintenance support
